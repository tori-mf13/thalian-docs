# Acceptable Use Policy

**Thalian, LLC**
**Effective Date:** March 2026
**Last Reviewed:** March 18, 2026

---

## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines the permitted and prohibited uses of the Thalian platform ("Service"). All users must comply with this policy in addition to our [Terms of Service](./terms-of-service.md).

## 2. Permitted Use

The Service is designed for IT teams to manage, monitor, and secure their organization's IT environment. Permitted uses include:

- Connecting your organization's identity providers, endpoint managers, and SaaS applications
- Reviewing AI-generated findings and security insights
- Taking remediation actions on accounts, applications, and devices within your organization
- Generating reports and tracking security posture over time
- Using the AI assistant to query your workspace data
- Exporting data for compliance, audit, or archival purposes

## 3. Prohibited Use

You may not use the Service to:

### Security and Abuse

- Attempt to access data belonging to other workspaces or tenants
- Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of the Service without authorization
- Interfere with or disrupt the Service or its infrastructure
- Circumvent rate limits, access controls, or security mechanisms
- Use automated tools to scrape, crawl, or extract data from the Service beyond its intended API

### Data and Content

- Upload malicious code, viruses, or harmful content
- Store data unrelated to IT infrastructure management
- Use the Service to process data you do not have authorization to access
- Connect integrations using credentials that do not belong to your organization

### AI Misuse

- Attempt to extract training data or model weights from the AI engine
- Use the AI assistant to generate harmful, abusive, or misleading content
- Manipulate AI prompts to bypass security controls or access controls
- Use AI-generated findings as the sole basis for legal or regulatory action without independent verification

### Legal and Regulatory

- Use the Service in violation of any applicable law or regulation
- Use the Service to facilitate unauthorized surveillance of individuals
- Process personal data in a manner inconsistent with applicable data protection laws

## 4. Resource Limits

- API rate limits and usage quotas are enforced per plan tier
- Excessive usage that degrades the Service for other users may result in throttling
- Free plan limits (25 identities, 3 integrations) are enforced automatically

## 5. Enforcement

Violations of this AUP may result in:

- Warning and request to correct the behavior
- Temporary suspension of access
- Permanent termination of the account
- Legal action where appropriate

We will attempt to notify you before taking action, except where immediate action is necessary to protect the Service or other users.

## 6. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any violation of this AUP, please report it to security@thalian.ai.
