# AI Brief, remediation approval workflow, and 3 new integrations

AI-generated security posture summaries, agent-initiated remediation approval workflow, sync events log, and three new integrations.

<!-- more -->

## AI Brief

AI-generated natural language summary of your workspace's security posture, available on the Dashboard and Reports pages.

## Remediation approval workflow

Agent-initiated actions on high/critical findings require Security Analyst approval before execution.

## Sync events log

View all data changes (created, updated, deleted) detected during integration syncs.

## New integrations

- **SentinelOne** — Agents, threats, and device health
- **Hexnode** — Cross-platform devices and policies
- **Jira Service Management** — Service requests, agents, and queues

## Improvements

- **MFA enforcement** — Workspace admins can now require TOTP-based MFA for all members
- **MTTR tracking** — Mean Time to Remediate metrics by severity, with trend charts
